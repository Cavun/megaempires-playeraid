Quick Reference

Phase Order

1
Tax Collection Simultaneous
  • 2 tokens per city from stock to treasury.
tap for card 👉
2
Population Expansion Simultaneous
  • +1 token in areas with 1 token (white side up as reminder action has already been taken).
  • +2 tokens in areas with 2 or more tokens.
  • Doubling is coincidence, not the rule. Example: +2 tokens in an area with 50 tokens.
  • Take census.
3
Movement Census Order
  • Tokens: 1 area by land or into a ship (and disembark later).
  • Maintain or buy boats at any time during your turn with 1 or 2 tokens from treasury or board.
  • A boat holds 5 tokens and moves 4 spaces.
4
Conflict Simultaneous
  • Token Conflict: 2+ players in an area exceeding the population limit.
  • Resolve by rounds of token removal, starting with the player with the fewest tokens (ties remove simultaneously).
  • City Conflict: a player has tokens in an area containing another player's city.
  • If attacker has 7 or more tokens: city converts to 6 tokens, then resolve token conflict. Otherwise nothing happens this phase.
  • Resolve token conflicts first, then city conflicts.
5
City Construction Simultaneous
  • Convert 6 tokens on a city site into a city, or 12 tokens on a non-city site into a city.
  • Remove surplus population.
  • Check city support: you must have 2 population per city. If not, reduce cities until you do, cities built this turn must be reduced first.
6
Trade Card Acquisition City Count Order
  • Receive 1 card from each stack up to your city count. (3 cities → a card from stacks 1, 2, and 3.)
  • In the same order cards were dealt, players may purchase 1 card from stack 9 for 15 tokens.
7
Trade Simultaneous
  • A trade involves at least 3 cards from both players.
  • The first 2 cards named in the transaction must be true.
  • Calamities may not be mentioned during trade.
  • The value of a set of the same commodity is printed at the bottom of each card.
8
Calamity Selection Simultaneous
  • If you hold more than 2 calamities, randomly discard down to 2.
9
Calamity Resolution Increasing Calamity Number Order
  • Resolve per the calamity reference sheet.
  • 1 token off the board = 1 damage, 1 city = 5 damage.
  • Reducing a city = converting it to population tokens equal to the population limit.
10
Special Abilities AST Progress Order
  • Resolve any special abilities granted by Advances.
11
Remove Surplus Population & Check City Support Simultaneous
  • Remove any surplus population tokens.
  • Check city support: 2 population required per city; reduce cities as needed.
12
Civilization Advances Acquisition Simultaneous
  • Purchase Advances using commodity cards and/or treasury tokens (each worth 1).
  • Cost = value in top left − credits from one of the cards colors − special credits from other card.
  • Take new credit tokens after buying a card.
  • IMPORTANT: Credit tokens are permanent, do not discard after using them.
13
AST Alteration AST Progress Order
  • Move a space along the AST if eligible.

⚠️ All Advances are listed in the phase(s) where they have an effect. Cards that only affect calamity resolution or only provide purchase credits will appear in Phase 9 or Phase 12 respectively. However, other cards also have an affect on Calamity Resolution. Check your Calamity Resolution Quick Chart for more info. All cards provide purchase credits.
